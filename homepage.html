Why Choose Us

Privacy Protected Camera

A privacy-protected is a feature that allows users to turn off their video feed or blur their background during a video conference to protect their privacy. This feature is important because it enables individuals to control the information they share during a video call and prevent others from seeing their surroundings or facial expressions.

Privacy protected Microphone

A privacy-protected microphone is a feature that allows users to mute their microphone or control their audio input during a video conference to protect their privacy. This feature is important because it enables individuals to control the information they share during a video call and prevent others from hearing their surroundings or private conversations.

Privacy Protected Chat

A privacy-protected chat is a feature that allows users to send messages to other participants during a video conference while protecting their privacy. This feature is important because it enables individuals to communicate without others seeing or recording their private conversations.
